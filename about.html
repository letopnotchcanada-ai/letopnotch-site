Our Story

A boutique wardrobe built around polish.

Le Top Notch is a refined womenswear destination on Laurier Avenue West in Outremont, shaped by a European sensibility, elevated styling, and a preference for pieces that feel timeless without feeling flat.

We carry a curated selection of premium Italian-made ready-to-wear, chosen for silhouettes that hold presence — softer neutrals, clean tailoring, graceful texture, and better wardrobe decisions. Our focus is clothing that feels elevated in real life, not just on the rack.

Since opening on Laurier Ouest, Le Top Notch has been part of one of Montréal's most distinguished fashion corridors — a street synonymous with considered taste, independent style, and the particular elegance of Outremont.

Each season, we refresh our edit with pieces that meet our standard: they have to earn their place. That means quality of construction, quality of feeling, and quality of longevity. We do not follow trends for trend's sake.

The Le Top Notch Woman

Composed. Considered. Quietly strong.

She is drawn to pieces with presence. She values quality, silhouette, and ease. She wants a wardrobe that feels composed without being overworked.

She buys less, but better. She invests in a jacket that lasts five seasons. She reaches for a dress that requires no explanation. She has no patience for fast fashion — not because of ideology alone, but because she simply has better taste.

The result is a collection built around dressing well with clarity: statement pieces, softer neutrals, tailored layers, and textures that add depth without excess.

Our Commitments

How we do business.

We believe a boutique has responsibilities — to the people who shop here, to the makers of the clothing, and to the neighbourhood it calls home. These are not marketing points. They are decisions we make every day.

🇮🇹

Italian Craftsmanship

We source primarily from Italian and European ateliers known for quality fabrics, skilled construction, and lasting silhouettes. We do not carry fast fashion.

♻️

Considered Volume

We buy in limited quantities intentionally. We would rather sell out than over-order. This means less waste and more meaning behind each piece.

🏘️

Local Roots

Le Top Notch is an independent boutique on Laurier Ouest. We are part of a neighbourhood, not a franchise. Every purchase directly supports a local business.

📦

Responsible Packaging

Our online orders ship in recyclable packaging. We avoid excess tissue paper and plastic inserts wherever possible.

Returns & Exchanges

Make a Return

We offer free, seamless returns on all online orders placed within Canada. Our policy is designed to be simple — if something is not right, we will fix it.

01

Request a Return

Email sales@letopnotch.com within 14 days of delivery with your order number and the reason for return. We will respond within 1 business day.

02

Send It Back

We will email you a prepaid return label. Package the item in its original condition with tags attached. Drop it at any Canada Post location.

03

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5 business days.

Conditions: Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached. Sale items and items marked "Final Sale" are non-returnable. Swimwear and undergarments are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

Exchanges: We are happy to exchange for a different size or colour if stock allows. Please mention this when contacting us so we can hold the item for you.

In-store purchases: In-store returns or exchanges can be made within 7 days with your receipt. Items must be unworn and in original condition.
